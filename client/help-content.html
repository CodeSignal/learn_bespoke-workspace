Workspace Overview

This workspace simulates a launch-day scenario for the Horizon v2.0 product. Use the four integrated apps below to manage the launch.

Email App

A full email client with folders, search, and compose.

  • Navigate between Inbox, Starred, Sent, Drafts, and Trash
  • Click an email to read it, then Reply, Star, or Delete
  • Click Compose to write a new email — start typing in the To field to pick from known contacts
  • Use the search bar to filter emails within the current folder

Pulse Chat

Instant messaging with your team members.

  • Sarah Chen — Engineering Manager, coordinating the launch
  • Alex Rivera — Senior Engineer, investigating production issues
  • Jordan Kim — Product Designer, dashboard & onboarding UX

Click a conversation, type a message, and press Enter to send. Teammates may reply automatically. Your messages can trigger events in other apps.

Docs Lib

Shared document viewer with inline editing and comments.

  • Single-click a section to open the comment panel on the right side
  • Double-click a section to edit it in place — press Escape to cancel, click outside or press Enter to save
  • Sections with comments show a blue left border and a count badge
  • The status table tracks launch milestones with color-coded statuses

Task Board

A Kanban board with four columns: Pending, In Progress, Blocked, and Done.

  • Click + in a column header to add a task with a title, description, and priority
  • Drag and drop cards between columns to update status
  • Click a priority badge (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) to cycle through levels
  • Priority is color-coded: red = High, orange = Medium, grey = Low

Tips

  • All data is saved in your browser's local storage and persists across refreshes
  • Your actions across apps are interconnected — chatting with a teammate or commenting on a doc may trigger responses in other apps
